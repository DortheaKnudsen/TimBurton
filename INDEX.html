TIM BURTON

The Good, The Bad, The Crazy

Tim Burton or Timothy Walter Burton was born in Burbank, California, to Jean Rae (Erickson), who owned a cat-themed gift shop, and William Reed Burton, who worked for the Burbank Park and Recreation Department. He spent most of his childhood as a recluse, drawing cartoons, and watching old movies. His movies are famous for their spooky and unique vibe, which always has the 3 main roles: the good, the bad and the crazy.
